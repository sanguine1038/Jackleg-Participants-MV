Summary

High-schooler Ranmaru Kageyama wakes up in an unknown location, unable to remember how they got there, and is forced to do fatal tasks. Soon they realize he finds out he's trapped with another group of 9 people to participate in a death game. Nothing could prepare Ranmaru for what he's about to experience. With risks around every corner and unwelcoming bunch, some might say they're too clumsy to be in such a game.

Participants

• Anzu Kinashi: High-schooler that has a clown side hussle. Bright and often scares themself over petty things.

• Alice Yabusame: Convict that was formerly apart of a visual kei rock band.

• Shunsuke Hayasaka: Nervous office aid that can't start a conversation nor hold one even if their life depended on it.

• Hinako Mishuku: Middle-schooler that isn't one to talk to others, very sassy.

• Kugie Kizuchi: High-schooler that got kidnapped with her little sister, often times just hanging out with the quiet people.

• Naomichi Kurumada: Obnoxious Boxer that's been on a losing streak.

• Mai Tsurugi: Baker that's not quite sure how she got where she is. Often friendly with other participants.

• Megumi Sasahara: Friendly policewoman. Chief of her own station and bringing justice of the little justice in the death game.

• Ranmaru Kageyama: High-schooler with a bit of a hidden side. Who knows who he really is?

• Shin Tsukimi: Shy Job-Hopper who often stutters and just follows what others tell them. Or maybe he's someone else entirely.
